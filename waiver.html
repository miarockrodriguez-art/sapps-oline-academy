CODE OF CONDUCT

By signing this waiver, the participant agrees to abide by the following Code of Conduct at all times while participating in SAPP'S O-Line Academy programs:

  • Show respect to all coaches, staff, and fellow athletes at all times
  • Follow all instructions and safety guidelines provided by coaching staff without exception
  • Refrain from any form of bullying, harassment, fighting, or unsportsmanlike behavior
  • Arrive on time and fully prepared for all scheduled training sessions
  • Report any injury, pain, or medical concern to coaching staff immediately
  • Maintain a positive attitude and demonstrate commitment to personal development
  • Treat all equipment and facilities with care and respect
  • Refrain from the use of drugs, alcohol, or any prohibited substances before or during sessions
