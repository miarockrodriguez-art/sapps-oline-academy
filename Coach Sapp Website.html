HAND TECHNIQUE
Training Tips · Coach Sapp
WHY HAND PLACEMENT WINS OR LOSES EVERY REP
The most overlooked fundamental in offensive line play is where you put your hands. Inside hands win. Thumbs up, elbows in, punch through the breastplate — that's not a suggestion, that's the law of the trenches. If your hands are outside, you've already lost the rep before your feet even move. Everything we teach at Sapp's O-Line Academy starts here.
By Coach Sapp
O-Line Fundamentals
FOOTWORK
Technique · Coach Sapp
FOOTWORK IS THE FOUNDATION — STOP SKIPPING IT
I see it every season — linemen with strong upper bodies and slow feet. You can be the strongest man on the field and still get beat every play if your footwork is sloppy. Kick slide, lateral shuffle, redirect — these aren't drills you do once and forget. They're movements you rep until they're instinct. At the Academy, footwork is non-negotiable from day one. Your feet are your power source.
By Coach Sapp
Pass Protection
MENTAL EDGE
Mindset · Coach Sapp
THE O-LINEMAN NOBODY TALKS ABOUT — THE MENTAL GAME
Offensive linemen don't get highlights. Nobody cheers when you dominate your block and open a hole. That's the position. You have to find motivation in the work itself — in knowing you controlled your gap, you protected your quarterback, you opened that lane. That internal scoreboard is what separates good linemen from elite ones. The grind is undefeated. Train your mind as hard as you train your body.
By Coach Sapp
